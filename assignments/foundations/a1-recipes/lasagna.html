Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Meat (example: lean ground beef)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Products:
    • Can of Crushed Tomato
    • Can of Tomato Sauce x2
    • Can of Tomato Paste x2
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning:
    • Fresh Parsley
    • Dried Basil Leaves
    • Salt
    • Italian Seasoning
    • Fennel Seeds
    • Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta Cheese
  • Egg

Lasagna Steps

  1. Make meat filling
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Make ricotta mix
  4. layer lasagna (see: Layering Lasagna)
  5. Preheat oven to 375F
  6. Cover in foil and bake (25 mins)
  7. Remove foil safely
  8. Bake without foil (25 mins)
  9. Let lasagna rest (~15 mins)
  10. Serve

Layering Lasagna

  1. Meat filling layer
  2. Noodle layer
  3. Ricotta mixture layer
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Paremsan cheese
  6. Repeat above layer steps
  7. Cover with remaining parmesan
